FAQ

What leaders ask before joining.

Are you a PMI Authorized Training Partner?

Yes. Our curriculum, materials, and instructors are approved through PMI’s ATP program, so your contact hours and certificates are automatically recognized.

How do the cohorts work?

Each Accelerator cohort runs for five weeks with live virtual workshops (recorded), async labs, mentor pods, and graded mock exams. Seats are capped at 30 to ensure meaningful feedback.

Do you offer corporate or team packages?

Absolutely. We customize onboarding, reporting, and leadership briefings for teams of 10+. Ask about private cohorts or executive intensives.

What support exists after certification?

Graduates remain in the Project Leaders Network, gain PDU micro-lessons, and can join the Leaders’ Roundtable or alumni mastermind tracks.
